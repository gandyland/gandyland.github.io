Projects

Here is where I am going to discuss my current and previous projects. Given that I have not done any I am just going to make some up.

'Mars Attacks' is an app I developed where the user is able to take control of the mars rover and give him directions for 5 minute periods at a time. They are then sent the corresponding data that NASA collected during their time in control. This app required special permission from NASA because it is basically a colossal waste of government funding.

'Players Gotta Play' is a mobile app developed for busy parents looking to set up playdates for their children. It lets parents know when other parents are bringing their children to public places, like parks and playgrounds, so the children can interact with their friends.

'AvoiDance' is a collaborative effort between myself and another developer that uses social media and location based services to avoid people you do not want to see in public for whatever reason. This app faced some initial issues regarding getting permissions from the user to agree to have themselves be avoided by people who did not want to see them as well as being able to use it to avoid other people. Eventually the users realized the benefits and 'AvoiDance' became a resounding success.
